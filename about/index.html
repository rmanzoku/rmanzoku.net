Positioning

中心テーマは、AI-BPO、AIエージェント運用、artifact-driven AI harness design、Forward Deployed Engineering、AI時代の業務設計です。

Profile in English

Ryo Manzoku is a Japanese technology executive and roving AI technical advisor (流しのAI技術顧問). After earning a master's degree in information science, he led SRE and infrastructure at mobile game and EdTech companies. He joined double jump.tokyo Inc. in 2018, became CTO in 2020, and Representative Director CTO in 2022. He founded Oasys Wallet Inc. in 2023 and has served as Director of Oasys Pte. Ltd. since 2025, after joining the gaming blockchain Oasys as Director in Tech in 2024. He currently works on AI-BPO and Forward Deployed Engineering, implementing generative AI in business operations and management.
